What supplies do I need?
You will need an LED nail lamp, a base/top coat, a colour base, rubbing alcohol, a nail file, nail glue, and nail tips.
Why do I need to file and clean my nails?
You need to file your nails in order to prevent them from showing underneath the nail tips. It is also recommended that you file the top of your nail a little bit to help the glue bond better. You need to clean your nails after so you have a dry surface free from oil and debris to make sure the nail tip and your nail are flush together.
How should I size my nail tips? In order to size your nail tips you need to make sure the nail fits from side to side and fits the curve of your cuticle.
How to pick a base and top coat? To pick a base or top coat you only really need a top coat. They are typically used as the same thing when doing nails, especially gel nails. When doing gel nails all you need to do is make sure the base/top coat (or colour) are led gel polishes. If they aren't the LED lamp won't cure them.
Where can I buy supplies? All supplies can be bought for a very beginner friendly price on Amazon.
