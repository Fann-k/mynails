In this page I will be showing you how to prep and apply false nails. As well as what supplies you will need and why they are important.

The supplies and tools you will need are:
LED Nail Lamp, Rubbing Alcohol, Nail Glue, False Nail Tips, Nail File, Clear Gel Nail Polish

STEP 1:

For step 1 you're going to file your natural nails down to a shape that allows them to not be seen underneath the nail tip. Example: if you have an almond tip file your natural nails to be a similar shape. After you do that you will need to lightly file the top of your nail and wash your hands thoroughly. Then use rubbing alcohol on top of your nail.
The reason why we file a little on top of the nail is to help the glue have grip on the nail. The rubbing alcohol ensures that you have no oils or residue on your natural nail that could impact how well the glue sticks.
